## 1. Foundation: Storage Integrity and NAS Architecture
Every agentic swarm is only as reliable as its data persistence layer. When building large-scale storage for AI training or RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), the physical hardware choice determines system latency and reliability.

* **Hardware Constraints**: Analyze why specific drive technologies fail under the heavy random-write loads of agentic databases in [Is SMR Hard Drive Tech Bad for NAS Storage Systems?](https://interconnectd.com/forum/thread/117/is-smr-hard-drive-tech-bad-for-nas-storage-systems/)
* **Strategic Note**: For 2026 high-availability clusters, prioritize CMR (Conventional Magnetic Recording) or enterprise NVMe to prevent the performance "cliff" associated with shingled recording.
