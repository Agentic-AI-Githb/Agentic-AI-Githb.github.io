* **NAS Performance**: Understand the risks of using shingled recording in AI-heavy environments in [Is SMR Hard Drive Tech Bad for NAS Storage Systems?](https://interconnectd.com/forum/thread/117/is-smr-hard-drive-tech-bad-for-nas-storage-systems/)
